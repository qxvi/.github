# qxvi Operational Doctrine

qxvi surfaces must remain public-only, inspectable, and bounded.

A qxvi surface may show a boundary condition.

A qxvi surface may expose a public frame, manifest, schema, or control object.

A qxvi surface must not claim evidence finality unless the evidence object is bound by the proper evidence system.

A qxvi surface must not claim system completion unless a completion object exists.

BOUNDARYCAM is the current primary surface.
